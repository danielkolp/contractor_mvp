# Euroflo — Caveman Version

> The clarity test. If the product can't be explained this simply, it isn't
> focused enough. This is the north-star statement; the full plan lives in
> `BUSINESS_PLAN.md`.

---

Contractor good at fixing pipe.

Contractor bad at chasing money.

Customer say: *"I need job."*

Contractor say: *"Okay, I send quote."*

Then customer disappear.

Contractor forget.

Money gone.

Bad.

---

## What Euroflo do

Euroflo say: *"Put job here. Put quote here. Put invoice here. Me watch money."*

- Customer fills form.
- Contractor makes quote.
- Customer accepts.
- Customer pays, or does not pay.

If customer does not pay, Euroflo bonk contractor on head and say:

> *"This money stuck. Chase now."*

That is app.

---

## Big simple idea

Euroflo is **not** giant contractor software.
Euroflo is **not** HomeStars.
Euroflo is **not** QuickBooks.
Euroflo is **not** big fancy CRM bullshit.

Euroflo is: **a tool that helps contractor not forget money.**

---

## Why contractor care

Contractor has many jobs. Jobs live in: texts, WhatsApp, notes app, brain,
random PDF, e-transfer messages, QuickBooks sometimes.

This is messy cave. **Money hides in messy cave.**

Euroflo makes clean cave.

---

## What money gets lost

- Quote sent, no reply.
- Invoice sent, no pay.
- Customer asks for job, contractor forgets.
- Deposit should be collected, but contractor forgets to ask.
- Old customer needs more work, but nobody checks.

Euroflo finds stuck money and says: *"This job not dead. Go poke customer."*

---

## Payment thing

Do not force Stripe down contractor throat. Contractor already likes e-transfer.

So Euroflo says: *"Use e-transfer. Use cash. Use cheque. Use card. Me no care.
Just mark job paid."*

This is smart. Because contractor thinks: *"Good. I don't have to change
everything."*

Then later, Stripe is optional convenience. Customer can click:
**"Accept quote and pay deposit."** That is useful. But not forced.

---

## How Euroflo makes money

**Main money: monthly subscription.**

- Free plan gets people in cave.
- Pro plan is $49/month.
- Team plan is $199/month.

Card fee is extra meat scraps, not main food. Many contractors still use
e-transfer, so if Euroflo depends only on Stripe fees, app starves.

**Subscription is meat. Transaction fee is seasoning.**

---

## Best selling line

Not: *"Euroflo is an all-in-one AI-powered contractor CRM platform."*
No. Bad. Too much SaaS cave paint.

Say: *"Euroflo helps contractors get paid by chasing stalled quotes and unpaid
invoices."*

Even simpler: *"Send quotes. Collect deposits. Chase unpaid jobs. Stop forgetting
money."*

---

## Rule for features

Every feature must answer: **"Does this help contractor get paid?"**

If yes, build. If no, throw rock at it.

**Build:** job request form · estimates · deposits · invoices · follow-ups ·
recovery queue · money-at-risk dashboard · mark paid by e-transfer/cash/card.

**Do not build yet:** reviews · ratings · marketplace · bidding · full scheduling
empire · giant CRM · fancy bullshit nobody asked for.

---

## The whole business in one caveman sentence

Contractor lose money because jobs, quotes, and invoices get forgotten. Euroflo
watches them, reminds contractor, helps customer pay, and charges contractor
monthly for less chasing and more paid jobs.

That's the business.
